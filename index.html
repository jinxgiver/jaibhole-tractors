About Us
Serving farmers of Agra & surrounding districts since 2008.

🚜 Jai Bhole Tractors – Kiraoli, Agra, U.P.

We are an authorised dealer of genuine tractor spare parts, proudly serving the farming communities of Agra, Mathura, Firozabad, and surrounding regions. Founded in 2008, our shop has grown from a small roadside store to one of the most trusted parts dealerships in western Uttar Pradesh.

We stock parts for all major tractor brands — including Sonalika, Eicher, Mahindra, John Deere, Swaraj, and New Holland. Every part we sell is genuine OEM or approved aftermarket quality, tested for reliability in Indian field conditions.

Our promise: Right part the first time. If we don't have it in stock, we'll source it within 48 hours.
